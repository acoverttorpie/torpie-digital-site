About

Torpie Digital is a family name. It’s on everything we build.

Torpie Digital is a small company run by Aiden Torpie, based in New York, working with local businesses everywhere.

The story

Small on purpose.

We started Torpie Digital because of a pattern we kept seeing: a local business pays for a website, the web person disappears, and the site slowly stops working. Nobody’s watching it. Nobody answers the reviews. Nobody notices when the Google listing breaks.

So we built a company where staying is the business model. We build your website, and then we take care of it: hosting, updates, edits, reviews, your Google presence. The build is a project. The care is the job.

Being small isn’t a stage we’re getting through. It’s the design. Your website is built and maintained by the same hands. When you email, the person who answers is the person who can fix it. No account managers, no tickets, no “let me check with the developer.”
